Volunteer
If you'd like to volunteer for the NC Rainbow Run please email us at ncrainbowrun@gmail.com
We need volunteers to be there on the race day (March 5th) before, during, and after the race. These volunteers will help set up, clean up, work at the registration tables, and work at water stations positioned along the race course. Also on the days leading up to the race we need volunteers to help spread the word and to help make finisher medals.
