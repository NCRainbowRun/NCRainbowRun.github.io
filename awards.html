Awards
While no awards will be given to the fastest runners (sorry, speedy people--but you'll still enjoy a great run at a fun place!), every finisher will get a rainbow-themed finisher medal and awards will be given in the following categories:
Top Three Individual Fundraisers: You loners out there will like this one. You'll be on a team of one. To be eligible for this award, you must check the "individual fundraiser" box on the registration form. The three individuals raising the most money for the NC Rainbow Run's list of charities (found in the Fundraising Competition tab at the top of the page) will be recognized at the post-race awards ceremony. You'll also win prizes! (But you will have to come out among people to claim your prize--hope that's not a problem!)
Top Three Fundraising Teams: For those of you who believe we're greater than the sum of our parts, compete as a team! To be eligible for this award you must check the "team fundraising" box on the registration sheet and include your team name, the names of all your teammates, and a link to your CrowdRise fundraising page. All team members must be registered for the race.
