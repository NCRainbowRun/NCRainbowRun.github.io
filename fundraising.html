Fundraising Competition
This race is all about raising money to support LGBT+ youth, so we'll be competing to see how much money we can all raise! Your registration money automatically goes to fund the Open House but by using our online partner CrowdRise you alone or with your team (of 3 to 10 runners) can raise additional funds for any of these charities:
  • The Point Foundation
  • Jim Collin's Foundation
  • The Astrea Lesbian Foundation for Justice
  • Ali Forney Center
  • Open House
To get started, gather your friends to form a team (or just inspire yourself if you're raising money as an individual) and create a fundraiser on Crowdrise. Be sure to say that you're running the NC Rainbow Run to raise money for the charity you've chosen from the list above. Share the name of your fundraiser on the registration form as well as the names of your teammates. Then, get busy on social media! Share the link to your fundraiser with all of your friends. Cajole them. Guilt them. Whatever. Just have fun while doing good and be good while having fun. For information on the charities listed above go to our Beneficiaries page.
