Sponsor the Run

Pot of Gold Sponsorship - $250

Roy G Biv Sponsorship - $100

The money from sponsors goes to purchasing race t-shirts, food for the runners, prizes, and decorations for the race. Every little bit helps towards strengthening our community and supporting and protecting our youth!
