Get signed up
To be a part of the race, you need to follow these steps:
  1. Fill out the registration form and mail with check to: Hoggard High School Gay-Straight Alliance, ATTN: Allison Collins, Faculty Advisor, 4305 Shipyard Blvd, Wilmington, NC 28403. The check should be made out to the Open House of Coastal Horizons.
  1. Follow @NC_Rainbow_Run on Twitter for updates and like us on Facebook
  1. If you'd like to participate in additional fundraising as an individual or a team, set up your account on Crowdrise following the directions on the registration form. Then start raising funds!
  1. You can also sign up on the day of the race. Registration begins at 7:30 am and we will only accept checks (made out to the Open House of Coastal Horizons) or cash.
