Get signed up
  1. If you'd like to participate in additional fundraising as an individual or a team, set up your account on CrowdRise and fill in the CrowdRise part of the form below when you buy your tickets. Then start raising funds!
  1. Follow @NC_Rainbow_Run on Twitter for updates and like us on Facebook
  1. You can also sign up on the day of the race (March 5th, 2016). Registration begins at 7:30 am and we will only accept checks (made out to the Open House of Coastal Horizons) or cash.
Pre-registration Form:
  1. How many tickets will you be buying?   
  1. Will you be competing in the Fundraiser Competition? This race is all about raising money to support LGBT+ youth, so we'll be competing to see how much money we can all raise! Your registration money automatically goes to fund the Open House but by using our online partner CrowdRise you alone or with your team (of 3 to 10 runners) can raise additional funds. You can read more about it on our website, under the Fundraising Competition tab. If you answer no now, but change your mind later, you can always email us at ncrainbowrun@gmail.com and we can add you to the competition!
